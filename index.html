Welcome to Your Journey

Please read everything carefully before starting your audition.

What is New Academy?

New Academy is the name of this audition program. It is not the final name of the group. We’re an independent, faith-based project created by people who love K-pop and want to use it to share a positive Christian message.

We are not a big label. We don’t have huge resources, big company backing, or a guaranteed fast debut. Things might move slowly. It may take time to grow, build a fanbase, and even to form the final group.

Because we’re small, there is no promise that this project will become super famous quickly or at all. We will do our best, work hard, and stay faithful—but success can be slow. If you join, you’re joining a journey, not just a result.

Who Can Join?

This audition is for boys ages 12–14 at the time of audition. We’re looking for people who:

  • Are passionate about music, performance, or visuals
  • Are willing to train, practice, and grow over several years
  • Are kind, respectful, and able to work in a team

You do not need to be perfect. You do need to be willing to learn, be corrected, and keep trying.

Faith & Mission

This is a Christian K-pop group project. Our songs and content will include themes of faith, hope, and God’s love. Members should be comfortable with Christian lyrics, prayer, and talking about faith.

  • We may decline events or opportunities that conflict with our values.
  • We care more about character and message than popularity.
  • We want to show you can be in entertainment while honoring God.

Training & Timeline

If you are chosen as a trainee, there may be a training period of 2–5 years before any official debut. Training could include:

  • Vocal practice / rap practice
  • Dance lessons and choreography training
  • Performance skills and stage presence
  • Content creation and social media activity

Training and being in a group is hard work—long hours, repetition, and high expectations. It can also be fun, rewarding, and life-changing if you’re truly passionate.

If the group debuts, we may ask for a multi-year commitment (for example, 5 years) so that we can grow, release music, and build a real community together.

Group Culture & Expectations

We want to build a family-style group, not a toxic or competitive environment.

  • There is no “king of the group.” Everyone matters.
  • Members must be kind, supportive, and encouraging.
  • Communication is important—if something is wrong, speak up.

No strict or unhealthy dieting will be required. Health and safety come first.

Social Media & Public Life

If you are chosen, you will need to be comfortable:

  • Having your face and name online
  • Posting content for fans
  • Being seen as a public figure

We’ll aim to guide you to use social media safely and wisely, but privacy will be different from a normal life.

Reality & Growth

This project is real, but it is also experimental and small. There is a chance:

  • We may grow slowly
  • We may not go viral quickly
  • Things may change over time

Please audition because you love the mission and the art, not just because you want fast fame.

Parents & Guardians

Parents/guardians must approve the audition and support the child during the process. Training, travel (if it happens), schedule changes, and online visibility all affect the family.

By submitting this audition, you confirm that a parent/guardian has read this information and agrees to be involved.
